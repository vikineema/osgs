Legend
----------------------------------------------

* Production ready services, indicated by the |PR SHIELD| badge, are known to be stable and reliable state.
* Work in progress, indicated by the |WIP SHIELD| badge, have some known integration issues and can be expected to have breaking changes implemented in the near future.
* Deprecated, or legacy services, indicated by the |DEP SHIELD| badge, are at end of life (EOL) and will not be supported in future releases.
* Deleted or removed services, indicated by the |DEL SHIELD| badge, are no longer included in the stack, although references and documentation may be of use.
* Gotchas indicate known issues, which either have known work arounds, might require significant effort to correct, or could even be labelled "won't fix". They may be indicated by the |EEK SHIELD| badge, with a colour intensity indicating the issue severity.
